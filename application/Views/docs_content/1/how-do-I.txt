# How do I change the site’s banner?

1. Tap “themes” in your admin panel.
2. Tap the currently selected theme.
3. Scroll down to the “site banner” input field.
4. Tap that button and select the new image on your computer.
5. Tap “save”.

Nerd : The database value is stored in site_milieu. It could be anything, including a remote file if you’re willing to hack it.

## How do I replace a comic page’s image?

1. Tap “chapter list” in your admin panel.
2. Tap to the chapter that contains the comic page in question.
3. Tap the “replace” input field and select the new image on your computer.
4. Tap the green “replace” button.

## How do I change my site’s favicons?

1. Look in /assets/images/system
2. Read about the different sizes at [Apple’s icon reference](https://developer.apple.com/library/ios/documentation/UserExperience/Conceptual/MobileHIG/IconMatrix.html)
